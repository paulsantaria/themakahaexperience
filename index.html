The Makaha Beach - miraflores - lima

The Makaha Beach whose waves are characterized by being of the Artificial Arrecife class, which are those that appear in both right and left and whose height exceeds the neighboring seaside resorts. In addition, its sea waves do not run in parallel with the coast but will break on the beach. This is why it is a seaside resort very popular by surfers and also by bodyboarders. In its surroundings we can also find sports clubs that remain open all year round. The length of this beacis approximately 300 meters.
